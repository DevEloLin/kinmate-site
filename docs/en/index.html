The real problems KinMate solves

Personal records shouldn't be scattered, unreadable, or locked inside someone else's server.

✕

Every family-record app dies the same way: data-entry friction. If you have to type out each item, dose and key value manually, you stop using it within a week — and an archive nobody maintains is worthless.

KinMate's AI does the typing. Snap a photo, get a structured record. The archive maintains itself because you're not maintaining it — you're just taking photos.

✕

Records scattered across paper, photos and a dozen hospital apps — never findable when the doctor asks.

One organized vault: every report, item and metric on a single timeline, searchable by person or pet.

✕

Foreign-language documents full of jargon you simply can’t decode.

AI explains any report in plain English or Chinese — so you understand before the appointment, not after.

✕

Caring for aging parents an ocean away, with no real sense of how they’re doing.

Track their medications and metrics, get a daily care check-in, and an alert the moment they miss one.

✕

Personal apps that quietly upload your most private data to their own servers.

Local-first, plus your own cloud. Zero personal data on our servers — encrypted with a key only you hold.

✕

Years of records trapped in an app that could shut down tomorrow.

You own every file. Export the whole vault anytime as an encrypted archive. No lock-in, ever.

✕

Forgotten pills and missed follow-ups across the whole household.

Smart local reminders for medications, vaccines, follow-ups and vet visits — for every family member and pet.
